Welcome to the Chief Legal Officer Diagnostic


This is a self-diagnostic that enables CLOs to assess their role and department. The diagnostic is for in-house legal departments only and is not appropriate for law firms.


The diagnostic covers three core areas:

  • Leadership and organisation of the department.
  • Department expertise and performance.
  • Working with the business and stakeholders.

Each of the above three areas has just 9 questions, each giving three statements and asking you to say which statement best reflects your currents state. This enables the diagnostic to give you a clear picture of your strength, weaknesses, opportunities, and threats.


The CLO Diagnostic is aliged to the 'CLO Management Toolbox' that looks at the Department Maturity and the 'CLO Department Diagnostic' that utilizing internal business unit feedback to get a business view point and indentify opportunities for improvement.
